Tu meri rooh da nasha pure obsession,
Main rakhda haan tainu like a confession.
Tere bullan da haasa driving me wild,
Main karda haan ishaara soft and mild.
Duniya di bheed vich hidden away,
Main labhda haan tainu every single day.
Assi dowein kalle haan out of sight,
Main tainu chahnda haan all through the night.
