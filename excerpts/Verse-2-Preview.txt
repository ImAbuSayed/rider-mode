Ik sau di raftar roaring machine,
Main chalaanda haan jazba feeling so clean.
Dhuan kadd diyan sadkan burning so fast,
Sadi ashiqui da safar meant to last.
Zeherila tera pyar toxic and sweet,
Main sehnda haan nakhre on my two feet.
Pith piche vaar ne fake friends around,
Main khada haan pakka holding my ground.
