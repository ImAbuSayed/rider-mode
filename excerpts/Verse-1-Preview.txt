Shehar diyan battiyan flashing bright lights,
Main labhda haan tainu through the dark nights.
Hawa vich ud’de waal wild and free,
Meri akhian ch vasdi just you and me.
Tatte lohe di thand cooling the heat,
Main karda haan wait on the empty street.
Nigghi sahaan di khushboo a secret sign,
Tainu apni banawanga cross the line.
