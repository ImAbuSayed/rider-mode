Kade lagda ae darr losing my mind,
Main labhda haan raste leaving behind.
Kaale shisheyan de piche hiding my tears,
Main larda haan kalla fighting my fears.
Teri kach wargi baanh delicate touch,
Main farda haan kaske holding so much.
Wafa meri sachi real and true,
Main jiyunda haan kudiye only for you.
